About Me

Personally, I grew up in the Pacific Northwest. I love to hike, travel, cook, and run with my dog Kodah. I dream of one day seeing the northern lights and hiking Macchu Picchu. Professionally, I graduated with a degree in American Studies with a focus in race and gender from the University of Mary Washington in 2016. I began my career at a sustainability nonprofit called Good360 as a Nonprofit Development Specialist. I am currently an Administrative Assistant with a trade association in Washington D.C. I dream of becoming a website developer, because I love to create.
